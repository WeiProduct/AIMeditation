Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

1. Overview

AI Meditation (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information Collection

The App follows the principle of minimal data collection:

  • Local Data Storage: All meditation records, mood data, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this data.
  • AI Chat Feature: When using the AI chat feature, your messages are sent through encrypted connections to our servers solely for generating responses and are not stored or used for other purposes.
  • No Registration Required: The App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

3. Information Use

We only use information in the following cases:

  • To provide AI meditation guidance services
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect user safety

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • AI conversations are not stored
  • Regular review and updates of security measures

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Used to provide AI chat functionality. OpenAI has its own privacy policy; please visit the OpenAI website for details.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data on your device at any time
  • Choose whether to use features that require internet connectivity
  • Contact us for more privacy-related questions

8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
