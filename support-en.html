Welcome to AI Meditation! We're committed to providing you with the best meditation experience. If you encounter any issues, please refer to the information below.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why does the AI chat feature require internet connectivity?

The AI chat feature uses OpenAI's GPT model to provide personalized meditation guidance. This requires sending your questions to our servers for processing. Rest assured, we do not store your conversation content.

2. What should I do if audio won't play?

Please ensure:

  • Your device is not in silent mode
  • The app has audio playback permissions
  • Try restarting the app

3. How do I switch languages?

In the app's settings page, you can switch between Chinese and English interfaces at any time. Your language preference will be saved locally.

4. Will my meditation records be uploaded?

No. All meditation records, mood data, and other information are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this data.

5. What permissions does the app need?

AI Meditation only requires the following permissions:

  • Network access (only for AI chat feature)
  • Audio playback permissions
